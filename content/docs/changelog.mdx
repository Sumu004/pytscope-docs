## v0.2.1

Amber-LED "hardware panel" terminal reports, a Training Efficiency Budget
anchored to MFU, exposed-communication analysis from `torch.profiler` traces,
zero-touch `AutoProfiler` instrumentation, and a full distributed vertical
(straggler detection, load imbalance, pipeline bubbles) — on top of the core
`Profiler`, the timing/memory/convergence/cross-signal analyzers,
Lightning/Hugging Face integrations, and the `analyze`/`diff` CLI.

Validated on a real multi-GPU NCCL run (Kaggle 2× T4) — see
[Validation](/docs/validation).
